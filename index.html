The Brave Little Tailor

Once upon a time, in a faraway land, there lived a tailor who was brave and clever. One day, he managed to kill seven flies with a single blow. Proud of his feat, he stitched a belt that read "Seven at One Blow" and set off to seek his fortune...

The Clever Fox

In a dense forest, a fox was known for his cunning ways. He could outsmart any animal and always found a way to get what he wanted. One day, he met a crow with a piece of cheese in its beak. The fox, thinking quickly, flattered the crow into singing, and as the crow opened its mouth, the cheese fell...

The Kind Woodcutter

Deep in the forest, a kind woodcutter lived with his family. One day, while chopping wood, he found a golden axe. Despite his poverty, he decided to return the axe to its rightful owner. His honesty was rewarded by the forest spirits, who granted him wealth and happiness...
